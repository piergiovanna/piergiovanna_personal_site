## For Prospective Students

If you are interested in working with me as a PhD student:

1. Apply to the [Stanford CS PhD program](https://cs.stanford.edu/admissions/phd)
2. Mention my name in your application
3. I will review all applications that mention my name

**Please do not email me asking about admission chances.** I receive hundreds of such emails and cannot respond to them individually.
