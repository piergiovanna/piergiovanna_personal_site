<h2 id="current-projects">Current Projects</h2>

### Digital Accessibility for Cultural Heritage

*2023–Present*

Research and development activities focused on improving accessibility to cultural heritage resources, digital collections and educational materials.

The project explores how accessibility standards, inclusive design methodologies and digital technologies can support broader participation in cultural heritage experiences and ensure equitable access to knowledge.

**Research Areas**

* Web accessibility
* Accessible digital collections
* Inclusive cultural heritage communication
* Accessibility evaluation methodologies
* User-centred design
* Accessible educational resources
* Accessibility and Artificial Intelligence

**Current Collaborations**

* Digital Arena for Inclusive Humanities (DAIH), University of Verona
* Cultural heritage institutions
* Digital Humanities researchers
* Wikimedia communities

---

### Accessibility and Inclusion in Digital Humanities

*2024–Present*

Research activities dedicated to the relationship between accessibility, inclusion and Digital Humanities.

The project investigates how digital methods, technologies and infrastructures can support more inclusive approaches to research, teaching and public engagement.

**Research Topics**

* Accessibility in Digital Humanities
* Inclusive digital scholarship
* Accessible educational technologies
* Diversity, accessibility and inclusion
* AI-assisted accessibility
* Accessible digital publishing
* Cultural participation and inclusion

---

### Accessible Cultural Heritage through Wikimedia and Open Knowledge Ecosystems

*2023–Present*

Research, outreach and capacity-building activities aimed at improving the accessibility, visibility and reuse of cultural heritage through Wikimedia projects and open knowledge ecosystems.

The initiative promotes collaboration among museums, archives, archaeologists, researchers and Wikimedia communities to support the dissemination of cultural heritage resources and foster public participation in knowledge production.

**Current Initiatives**

* Wikipedia e Archeologia
* GLAM – Musei Civici di Reggio Emilia
* GLAM – Museo Veneto del Giocattolo
* Wikimedia-based cultural heritage outreach activities

**Research and Development Areas**

* Open cultural data
* Public archaeology
* Cultural heritage communication
* Community engagement
* Digital storytelling
* Open access to cultural heritage resources
* Collaborative knowledge production
* Wikimedia platforms (Wikipedia, Wikidata and Wikimedia Commons)
