<h2 id="projects">Projects & Collaborations</h2>

Coordination and participation in research and cultural heritage projects focused on Digital Humanities, archaeological data management, digital accessibility, GLAM initiatives, and open knowledge.

### Wikimedia, GLAM and Open Knowledge

* **Wikipedia and Archaeology** (2023 – present): creator and coordinator of the GLAM project funded by Wikimedia Italia, in collaboration with the Soprintendenza Archeologia, Belle Arti e Paesaggio for the provinces of Verona, Rovigo, and Vicenza.

* **GLAM – Musei Civici di Reggio Emilia** (2026 – 2027): creator and coordinator of a Wikimedia Italia-funded GLAM initiative in collaboration with the Civic Museums of Reggio Emilia, aimed at improving public access to museum collections through open digital platforms.

* **GLAM – Museo Veneto del Giocattolo** (2025 – present): coordination of Wikimedia-based activities for the dissemination and enhancement of museum collections.

* **Accessibility in Wikimedia Projects** (2024 – present): research, training, and experimentation activities focused on digital accessibility, inclusive participation, and the reduction of barriers within Wikimedia projects.

### Digital Humanities and Cultural Heritage

* **DAIH – Digital Arena for Inclusive Humanities**: collaboration with the University of Verona on digital accessibility, inclusion, and digital technologies for the humanities.

* **Archaeological GIS of Rubiera** (2019 – 2022): implementation and update of the Archaeological Territorial Information System and coordination of archaeological field survey activities.

* **SITAVR – Archaeological Territorial Information System of Verona** (2011 – 2017): head of IT infrastructure and research fellow for archaeological data modelling, interoperability, and mapping to CIDOC CRM and Getty Art & Architecture Thesaurus standards.

* **ARIADNE Research Infrastructure**: contact person for the partnership between the SITAR project in Rome and the European ARIADNE consortium for archaeological data interoperability.
